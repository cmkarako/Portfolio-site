About Me

I have always been fascinated by code and started building projects to satisfy my desire to learn how it all works.

My curiosity has led me down various paths - leading me as a scientist and researcher. From nanoscale thin films to automotive parts, I enjoy the process of digging for answers. When a coworker mentioned that I should code, I was both intrigued and intimidated! I dove right in and never looked back. My favorite part of coding is trying to break my own code, try to find the problems, and get myself into a huge mess. But it's always worth it when I find the root of the issues and end with cleaner code than I started with!
